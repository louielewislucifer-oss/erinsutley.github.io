My Story

I've never been someone who followed a straight line.

I've spent my career building and scaling organizations, but my interests have always stretched well beyond the role I happened to hold. From analyzing financial markets to the fast-moving New York e-commerce scene — I'm just as likely to spend my evenings researching the future of aging, absorbing content on quantum physics, or exploring how AI will reshape the human experience.

I'm endlessly curious about how people and systems work together, and the innovation that becomes possible when they do. Whether I'm helping a company navigate change or building something from scratch, I'm driven by curiosity and a desire to discover what brings out the best in people, ideas, and organizations.
